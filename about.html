About Me

Hi, I'm Anna and I am UX engineer from England currently living and working in SF.

After graduating school I did a Ba Hons in Fine Art Ceramics. Since then I have worked in events management, outdoor sports retail, CRUD scripting, frontend development and more.
I have lived in four countries: England, Wales, Australia and San Francisco, America which has fueled my love of travel as well as a love for nature and hiking. My recent goal in life has been to reduce the amount of everyday plastic and other packing that I use everyday.
